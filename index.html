The Museum of Fine Arts

The Museum of Fine Arts, often recognized as the Boston Museum of Fine Arts, is a Boston national artifact and one of the largest art museums in the world thanks to its well-balanced collection. The Boston Athenaeum library's art holdings served as the foundation of the museum's initial collection, when it opened its doors in 1870.
